BIO.

Adit is a product designer based in Bangalore, India.


He is currently a senior designer at Flipkart - India’s largest e-commerce player. He leads the design on core experiences like Cart, Checkout and Payments on the app. He is also the designer behind the latest website experience.


Previously, he was a lead designer at Housing.com - A leading online real estate company. He was majorly responsible for the Android/iOS app experiences for the home rentals business. Before that he was a designer/engineer at Ricoh Innovations where he worked on apps built on top of their visual search technology.


He has also published his UX research work in the Asia Pacific CHI’13 and is a co-inventor in many patents around visual search.


He loves’s doing side-projects. He’s the creator and designer of Skim - A Flipboard for adventures in your city. Skim got featured inside travel on the App store. Qwick, a fun iOS app for quick feedback. It ran on alpha for top product companies in India.


In his free time he writes his own bio in third person. :)
